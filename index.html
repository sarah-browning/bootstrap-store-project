About Us

Why hello and welcome to Dice, Dragons & Documents. We're a little fictional company created in 2021 for Sarah Browning's Bootstrap Project. We specialize in RPG sourcebooks, supplements, and accessories. Inspired by Sarah's love of TTRPGs and websites such as the DMsGuild, Roll20 Marketplace, and Drive-ThruRPG, we hope you enjoy taking a look around. We ship our products from our imaginary warehouse in New Glasgow, Nova Scotia to the rest of Canada with help from Sarah's three cats.
